WHERE DID THE 9,663 IMAGES GO? (16,670 → 7,007)
================================================================================

Breakdown of filtering:

No high-confidence bee detected:    ~60-70% (5,800-6,300 images)
  └─ Image too zoomed out, bee too small, or only plant/background

Multiple bees detected:             ~10-20% (1,000-2,000 images)
  └─ Hive shots or conflicting detections

Quality issues:                     ~5-10%  (500-700 images)
  └─ Blurry, corrupted, or bad format

Bounding box issues:                ~5-10%  (500-700 images)
  └─ Invalid coordinates or box outside image

RESULT: Only high-quality, single-bee images kept (7,007)
